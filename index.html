"Strawbery Shortcake"

"Strawberry Shortcake" is a sweet but spunky little redhead with enough optimism to fill a strawberry field. She believes things are growing better all the time and as the unofficial berry princess of Strawberryland, she puts her heart and soul into seeing that things improve for everyone she meets. She shares her berry bungalow with her baby sister Apple Dumplin', her sarcastic cat Custard, and her mischievous dog named Pupcake. Strawberry is also quite adventurous, so it's no surprise that she has friends from other lands both near and far.
